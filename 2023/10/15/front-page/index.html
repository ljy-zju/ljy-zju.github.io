About me

  Welcome to my page! I am currently a dedicated second-year graduate student at IASLAB, a vibrant research hub within the College of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University. Under the supervision of Prof. Yu Zhang, I’m delving into the intriguing intersection of Robotics and Computer Vision. My academic path began with a BS degree from The Mixed Class at Chu Kochen Honors College, where I majored in Automation, laying a strong foundation for my current research endeavors.

   My current research areas, summarized as E-S-L-A-M, involve:

  • Event camera SLAM
  • Surface reconstruction using visual-based tactile sensors
  • LiDAR localization with prior maps.
  • Autonomous UAV navigation.
  • Motion estimation in wheeled bipedal robots.
