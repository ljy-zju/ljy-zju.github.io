About me

  I am a second-year graduate student at IASLAB, part of the College of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Yu Zhang. I received my BS degree from The Mixed Class in Chu Kochen Honors College, majoring in Automation within the College of Control Science and Engineering at Zhejiang University.

   My primary research focus lies in the field of Robotics and Computer Vision. Specific research areas of interest encompass E-S-L-A-M:

  • Event camera SLAM.
  • Surface reconstruction using visual-based tactile sensors.
  • LiDAR localization based on prior maps.
  • Autonomous flight of UAV.
  • Motion estimation for wheeled bipedal robots.
